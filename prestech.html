ABOUT US

PresTech is an electronic accessories developer based out of San Francisco,  California. We were formed in 1982 by our sole founder and former CEO, William Preston. We often work with other technology companies like Apple and Sony to ensure that our products are compatible with theirs.

We only have one main headquarters have in San Francisco, located in the same building where we were founded, but we also have several smaller offices located around the world, including locations in Tokyo, Dubai, and Oslo. Our company is diverse and a welcoming space, as we hope to hire the best and the brightest to add our ranks.

We are committed to excellence here at PresTech.
